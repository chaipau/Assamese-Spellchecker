This dictionary is based on a word list of around 20,000 Assamese words that
have been collected from various articles and magazines, blogs and
translations that have been done by Amitakhya Phukan and which are available
for any one's use. Major contribution has come from Mr. Navanath Saharia of
Tezpur University.
